Measuring Geopolitical Risk: Replication Material

Last update: October 20, 2021. Matteo Iacoviello.

This page contains the files to reproduce the results in "Measuring Geopolitical Risk" as well as any additional documentation referred in the paper.

For each section below, download the entire set of files in that section to run the necessary scripts

For questions or comments, please contact me at iacoviel@gmail.com or matteo.iacoviello@frb.gov

Cite as: Iacoviello, Matteo and Dario Caldara (2021), "Replication Material for Measuring Geopolitical Risk," available at https://www.matteoiacoviello.com/gpr_replication.htm

The paper is available here. Updated data can be found on the geopolitical risk webpage
